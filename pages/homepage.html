Roro

This is Roro. He is a 7 years old mix breed. He has all the needed vaccinations and a passport. His weight is around 10kg. Roro is a gentle and sweet soul that loves hugs and is always happy to see you. If you wish to donate or adopt Roro visit the How to adopt button!

Delta

This girl is 4 years old Delta. She is a mix breed weighing 20kg. She has been vaccinated, has a passport and a chip. Delta is still a crazy baby dinosaur running around trying to get your attention. She is very sweet and really smart so she can't be fooled with silly tricks. If you wish to donate or adopt Delta visit the How to adopt button!

Kety

This is 16 years old Kety. She is a Thai cat. She has been vaccinated and has a passport. Kety is an old lady and a grumpy one. She can be a tough nut to crack and is very moody but if you feed her and give her attention ONLY when she wants she's a sweetheart. If you wish to donate or adopt Kety visit the How to adopt button!
