Achievements

Since the launch of the shelter, we've made significant strides in improving the lives of our animals, engaging our community, and expanding our reach. Below are some of the key achievements we've accomplished so far:

Rescued over 200 animals in the past year

Successfully facilitated the adoption of 300 animals

Expanded the volunteer program, doubling the number of active volunteers to 100

Established a foster program that placed 150 animals in temporary homes

Raised 30,000 lv through various fundraising initiatives

Provided medical treatment services to 300 animals

All these achievements have cause a significant impact on the improving of the shelter and the conditions for the animals. Our impact summed up:

  • Saved animals from abusive environments, natural disasters, and abandonment, providing them with a safe and caring home.
  • Found loving and permanent homes for animals, significantly reducing shelter overcrowding.
  • Enhanced animal care and shelter operations with the help of dedicated volunteers.
  • Improved the health and well-being of the shelter animals and helped control the animal population.
  • Provided a safe and loving environment for animals until they were ready for adoption.
  • Secured essential funds to cover operational costs, medical expenses, and facility improvements.
